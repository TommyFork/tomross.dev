## tomross.dev

My personal site — a place to share what I've worked on, how to reach me, and a bit about who I am.

The site is minimal by design. A few pages: work I'm proud of, a way to get in touch, and a short about. No blog, no feed, no noise.

Animations are intentional but restrained. The contact flow has a small amount of care put into it. Email is protected from scrapers without hiding it from real people.
